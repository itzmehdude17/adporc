Sciatica Pain: Causes, Symptoms & Physiotherapy Treatment in Jatrabari, Dhaka | ADPORC

Sciatica pain causes sharp discomfort that travels from the lower back through the hip and down the leg, often accompanied by tingling or numbness. The pain may worsen after sitting for a long time, sudden twisting, or standing for extended periods. Many patients feel increased pain while walking, bending forward, or trying to stand up from a seated position. At ADPORC in Jatrabari, Dhaka, we identify the root cause of sciatica and focus on reducing nerve compression, controlling pain, and restoring normal mobility through evidence-based physiotherapy care.

Under the direct supervision of Dr. Saddam Hossain, our sciatica pain treatment focuses on reducing nerve pressure, restoring hip mobility, and improving leg strength through safe clinical physiotherapy.

What is Sciatica Pain?

Sciatica pain is a type of nerve pain that radiates from the lower back down to the buttock, thigh, leg or even the foot. The main cause is compression or irritation of the sciatic nerve, the longest nerve in the human body.

Sciatica is not a disease by itself, it is a symptom of an underlying problem such as PLID, disc bulge, muscle spasm or spinal degeneration.

Without timely treatment, sciatica can become a persistent source of pain and may gradually affect your walking pattern, posture, and overall lower limb function often leading to further complications in the back, hip, or leg.

Common Symptoms

People with sciatica pain commonly experience:

  1. Sharp or burning pain down the leg
  2. Pain increases during sitting, bending, sneezing or coughing
  3. Numbness or tingling in the leg
  4. Weakness during walking
  5. Difficulty standing straight
  6. Lower back stiffness

Major Causes of Sciatica

  1. PLID (Prolapsed Lumbar Intervertebral Disc)
  2. Disc bulge or herniation
  3. Piriformis muscle tightness (Piriformis Syndrome)
  4. Spinal stenosis
  5. Spondylolisthesis
  6. Muscle spasm & poor posture

Ignoring early signs can lead to chronic low back pain and gait abnormalities.

How Physiotherapy Helps?

At ADPORC, our treatment does more than reduce pain it addresses the root cause. Our evidence-based physiotherapy includes:

  1. Pain relief modalities
  2. Advanced electrotherapy
  3. Nerve mobilization
  4. Spinal mobilization / manipulation

  6. Posture correction

Our goal is to help you walk pain-free again quickly and safely.

Home Advice

  1. Avoid soft bed
  2. Avoid long sitting
  3. Use lumbar support
  4. Gentle stretching
  5. Avoid lifting heavy objects

When to See a Physiotherapist (Visit ADPORC)?

Seek physiotherapy if you notice:

  1. Pain lasting more than 7-10 days
  2. Pain radiating below the knee
  3. Difficulty walking
  4. Numbness or weakness
  5. Pain disturbing sleep
  6. Repeated back pain episodes

Untreated sciatica can lead to persistent low back and leg pain, altered walking mechanics, and may eventually require more invasive interventions. Early physiotherapy ensures the best chance for full recovery and pain‑free mobility.

সায়াটিকা পেইন কী?

সায়াটিকা পেইন হলো এক ধরনের নার্ভ পেইন যা কোমর থেকে শুরু করে নিতম্ব, উরু, পা বা পায়ের পাতায় পর্যন্ত চলে যায়। এর মূল কারণ হলো সায়াটিক নার্ভ-এর চাপ পড়া বা জ্বালা সৃষ্টি হওয়া।

সায়াটিকা নিজে কোনো রোগ নয়, বরং এটি একটি উপসর্গ। সাধারণত PLID, disc bulge, পেশির টান, বা স্পাইনাল ক্ষয়ের কারণে এই ব্যথা হয়।

সময়মতো চিকিৎসা না হলে সায়াটিকা দীর্ঘস্থায়ী ব্যথায় রূপ নিতে পারে এবং ধীরে ধীরে হাঁটার ধরন, posture ও পুরো পায়ের কার্যক্ষমতায় প্রভাব ফেলতে পারে ফলে কোমর, নিতম্ব বা পায়ে অতিরিক্ত জটিলতা দেখা দেওয়ার ঝুঁকি বাড়ে।
