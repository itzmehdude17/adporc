Understanding Back Pain:

Back pain can happen because of bad posture, muscle strain, lack of movement, or spine problems like slipped discs or arthritis. If early signs are ignored, the pain can get worse and affect daily life.

What Causes Back Pain?

Back pain can arise from several common causes:

  1. Poor sitting or sleeping posture
  2. Sedentary lifestyle or lack of movement
  3. Muscular strain or ligament injury
  4. Spinal disc problems (e.g., PLID)
  5. Arthritis or age-related wear and tear

Ignoring early symptoms can lead to chronic pain and difficulty performing daily tasks.

How Physiotherapy Helps Back Pain

At ADPORC, we focus on treating the root cause, not just the symptoms. Our comprehensive physiotherapy for back pain includes:

  1. Manual Therapy: Joint and soft tissue mobilization
  2. Electrotherapy: TENS, ultrasound, IFT for pain relief
  3. Targeted Exercises: Core and lumbar strengthening
  4. Posture Training: Ergonomic guidance for daily life
  5. Patient Education: Prevention tips and lifestyle modification

At ADPORC, Dr. Saddam and his team tailor plans for your specific condition and lifestyle, ensuring safe and effective recovery.

Home Exercises You Can Try:

Our therapists often suggest the following beginner exercises:

  1. Cat-Camel Stretch: Enhance spine flexibility
  2. Pelvic Tilts: Strengthen lower back
  3. BBridge Exercise: Activate glutes and supports core and relieves stress on spine

Perform 10 to 15 reps, twice a day. Keep in mind, always warm up and consult your physiotherapist before starting any new routine.
