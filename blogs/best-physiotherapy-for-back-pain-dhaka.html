Back pain is one of the most common reasons people visit a Physiotherapist. At ADPORC in Dhaka, under the guidance of Dr. Saddam Hossain, we specialize in designing personalized treatment plans to relieve pain, restore mobility, and strengthen your back muscles.

Understanding Back Pain:

Back pain can happen because of bad posture, muscle strain, lack of movement, or spine problems like slipped discs or arthritis. If early signs are ignored, the pain can get worse and affect daily life.

How Physiotherapy Helps?

Physiotherapy addresses the root causes with:

  1. Hands-on therapy: For muscle and joint mobilization
  2. Targeted exercises: For core-strengthening, stretching
  3. Ergonomic training: For improving posture
  4. Electrotherapy: For pain relief and muscle stimulation
  5. Patient Education: On lifestyle changes to prevent recurrence
At ADPORC, Dr. Saddam and his team tailor plans for your condition and lifestyle.

At-Home Exercises You Can Do:

  1. Cat-Camel Stretch: Enhance spine flexibility
  2. Pelvic Tilts: Strengthen lower back
  3. Bridge Pose: Activate glutes and support spine
Perform 10 to 15 reps, twice daily. Always warm up first.
