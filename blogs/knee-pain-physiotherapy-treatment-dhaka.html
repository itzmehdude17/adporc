Knee pain can limit your freedom from walking up stairs to sitting down for namaz. At Asia Digital Physiotherapy & Orthopedic Rehabilitation Center (ADPORC), we provide gentle yet effective physiotherapy to reduce pain, improve joint function, and help you move confidently again.

Under the expert care of Dr. Saddam Hossain, PT, a certified physiotherapist based in Jatrabari, Dhaka, our knee pain treatment combines manual techniques, electrotherapy, and structured exercises for conditions like arthritis, ligament strain, or post-surgery rehab.

Understanding Low Knee Pain

Knee pain can result from injury, wear and tear, or inflammation. Common conditions include osteoarthritis, patellar tendinitis, meniscus tears, and postural imbalances. If not treated early, it can limit mobility and worsen over time.

Common Causes of Knee Pain

Knee pain can arise from various factors, including:

  1. Age-related wear (e.g., arthritis)
  2. Ligament sprain or meniscus injury
  3. Poor posture or leg alignment
  4. Previous trauma or surgery
  5. Weak thigh or hip muscles

How Physiotherapy Helps

At ADPORC, we focus on relieving pain and restoring function through:

  1. Manual therapy to mobilize the joint and soft tissue
  2. Ultrasound, TENS or IFT for swelling and pain
  3. Stretching and strengthening thigh and calf muscles
  4. Gait training and posture correction
  5. Personalized rehab plans post-ACL or meniscus surgery

Our goal is to help you regain full range of motion and strength, so you can return to daily activities without pain.

Home Exercises to Try

  1. Quad sets: Tighten and strengthen thigh muscles
  2. Heel slides: Improve range of motion
  3. Straight leg raises (SLR): Activate knee-supporting muscles
Perform 10-15 reps, twice a day. Keep in mind, always warm up and consult your physiotherapist before starting any new routine.
